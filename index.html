About us

Our business model is simple: forget the high scores, and high rankings. Here you play to lose! And your loses will lead to a better rank in our system.

We cannot change the ranks used in bowling computers, but our calculators can adjust your overall scores based on how poorly you play, and ultimately the players with the worst scores will be at the top of their divisions.

Our players have indicated how much they enjoy playing for the sake of losing, because it completely changes their mindset on bowling and creates an even more challenging game because you have to play worse than your competition.
