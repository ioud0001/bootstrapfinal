Locations

Atlantic Canada | The Prairies | Central Canada

We have a number of locations across Canada!

If your location is not listed, do not worry! We only list the main bowling offices on our website, but we have leagues in smaller cities as well. The smaller city leagues compete against each other in the vicinity of a major city and then it is determined who will proceed to the next round.

During tournaments with other divisions, teams play in any of the major cities nearest to their home town and the scores are combined in real-time.

During the semi-finals between divions, players of winning teams will have the opportunity to travel.
