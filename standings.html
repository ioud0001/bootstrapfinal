Current Standings

With our reverse-ranking system a loss in a regular bowling league is a win for you!

The goal is to play as terribly as possible.

Our bowling alleys use "gutter-guards", to prevent players from consistently throwing their ball into the gutter, which would give you a high score and is considered cheating in our leagues.

Professionals are welcome also: playing for the purpose of losing can lead to some interesting scores!

The fun part about our league is some of us forget that we're playing to lose, which leads to some interesting scores. Others find confidence in our sport and gain better self-esteem.
